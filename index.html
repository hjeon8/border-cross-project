WHAT'S THE REASON?

Reason behind pattern

Above graph is number of travel from Canada and Mexico to the US.
While analyzing by measure of crossing, personal vehicle got affected by month more than any other measures and it has highest number of crossing among all measures. Since personal vehicle is highest measure for border crossing, there was a hypothesis that it might be related to travel/tourism.
As a result, February has the lowest volume of travel and August has the highest volume. Therefore, there is high possibility border crossing amount is affected by volume of travel.

Border Crossing Prediction
